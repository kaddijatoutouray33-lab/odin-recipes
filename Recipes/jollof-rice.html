Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a beloved West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and spices. It's vibrant, flavorful, and often served at celebrations and family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain rice
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 fresh tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes, onion, and pepper into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil and fry the tomato paste until reduced.
  3. Add seasonings and stir well.
  4. Add washed rice and water, then cover to cook.
  5. Simmer until rice is soft and flavorful.
