Domoda

Description

Domoda is The Gambia’s national dish—a rich peanut stew made with meat or fish, vegetables, and groundnut paste. It's hearty, comforting, and deeply satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef or chicken
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 potato
  • 1 carrot
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Brown meat in a pot and set aside.
  2. Sauté onions and tomatoes until soft.
  3. Add peanut butter and water to create a thick sauce.
  4. Add meat and chopped vegetables.
  5. Simmer until everything is tender and flavors are well combined.
